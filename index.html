Student Registration System

Manage Your Student Records Seamlessly!

Useage:-

            *This system allows you to add, edit, and delete student records.
             ↪The student name field can only contain alphabets.
             ↪The student ID field can only contain numbers.
             ↪ The email field must contain a valid email address, including the ‘@’ symbol.
             ↪ The contact number field can only contain numbers and must be exactly 10 digits long.
            *The data is stored locally and persists even after refreshing the page. 
            *You can edit existing records, and edited cells will be marked with an  E  symbol.
            *If edit-mode On The opted Field background-color Change to PINK.
